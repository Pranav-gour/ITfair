Superconducting Quantum Computers

Superconducting Quantum Computers are currently the most popular approach for making Quantum Computers. They consist of Superconducting Qubits which are made up of Superconducting Wires with breaks in the Superconductor known as Josephson Junction. The most popular type of Superconducting Qubit is called a Transmon Qubit, where the 2 energy level system is encoded in pairs of electric charge, moving across the junction, specifically the frequency at which charges oscillate back and forth across the junction.

Superconducting Materials

  • Niobium
  • Mercury
  • Lead
  • Technetium
  • Carbon Nanotubes (Organic Material)
